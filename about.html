About Le Crest Hospital

Le Crest Hospital, a 250 Bedded Super Specialty Hospital and Oncology Centre in Sector 4, Vasundhara, Ghaziabad, is expected to start functioning in mid 2019. It is the brain child of renowned doctors, Dr. Sanjay Garg, Mr. Pankaj Agarwal,Dr. Charu Garg & Mr. Deepak Gupta They are Chairman and Chief Promoters of the hospital.
